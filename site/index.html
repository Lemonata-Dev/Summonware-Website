FAQ

What is Summonware?

An AI-forward software house. We replace your need for an agency, a dev team, a design studio, and an AI consultancy — one partner that takes an idea to a shipped product with pro-level quality and AI-accelerated delivery built in.

Who is Summonware for?

Anyone with a product to build. Whether the idea has been on your whiteboard for months or you ship every week — we make building faster, calmer, and more creative. Bring the idea; we handle the setup hassle.

How is this different from a regular agency?

Most agencies just execute tickets. Summonware also scopes the product, designs the journey, wires in AI where it earns its keep, and stays for launch and growth. And it all comes from one senior team.

How does the AI-accelerated pipeline work?

Every task runs through our internal tooling built on frontier models — code generation, review, test synthesis, and docs. Humans make the calls; the pipeline removes the grind. You get senior output at roughly twice the pace.

What's the turnaround?

First working demo in about two weeks including scoping and design. The discovery-to-quote step itself is around five days — most founders tell us that's faster than their last team's sprint planning.

Will the AI affect my code quality?

Unreviewed AI code tends to be verbose and brittle — so ours never ships unreviewed. Every line passes senior review, tests, and static analysis before it reaches your repo.
